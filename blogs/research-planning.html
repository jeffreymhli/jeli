How I Plan Research Projects

A good project starts from one clear question, one measurable success metric, and one realistic delivery timeline. I avoid broad goals early and instead define one minimum useful experiment.

Checklist

  • Define exact hypothesis and expected signal.
  • Write baseline setup before starting novelty work.
  • Track every run with reproducible configs.
  • Set a stop condition to avoid endless iteration.
